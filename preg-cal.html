FAQ

Common questions

How accurate is a conception calculator?

It’s an estimate. Accuracy depends on how regular your cycles are and whether ovulation timing differs. Early ultrasound dating is usually more accurate for due date estimates.

What if my cycle is irregular?

Select Irregular / Not sure. The tool will widen the fertile window and conception range. If you track ovulation (OPKs, BBT), use those to refine your estimate.

Can I use this for IVF/IUI cycles?

This calculator is designed for natural cycles. For assisted reproduction, due dates are often calculated from transfer date and embryo age—ask your clinic for the best method.
