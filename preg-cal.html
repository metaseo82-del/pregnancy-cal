FAQ

Common questions

How accurate is a conception calculator?

It is an estimate. Accuracy depends on cycle regularity and when ovulation actually occurred. Early ultrasound is often more accurate for dating a pregnancy.

What if my cycle is irregular?

Select Irregular / Not sure. The tool will widen the fertile window and conception range. Ovulation tracking methods may help refine the estimate.

Can I use this for IVF or IUI cycles?

This calculator is designed for natural cycles. IVF and IUI dating is often based on transfer or procedure date and embryo age, so clinic guidance is preferred.
